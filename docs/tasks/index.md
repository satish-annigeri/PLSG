# Task Lists

The list of tasks are meant to help members of the group in being ready to attend the upcoming online session. Completing the tasks will help in reducing the time spent in giving instructions, and thereby spending more time on addressing difficulties being faced.

The tasks are specific and clear in what the members are expected to do. They can refer to the [resources](../resources/index.md) section of this web site or any other source they prefer to complete the tasks.
