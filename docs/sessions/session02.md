## Why program computers?
The primary reason of programming computers is to automate repetitive tasks, especially those that take long time and may result in human errors. Human errors are those that occur not because of lack of knowledge but due to oversight, fatigue or monotony. Tasks that do not requirement human judgement are well suited for automation. But to decide which task requires human judgement is subjective.
